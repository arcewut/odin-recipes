Completos

Description

Completo, a traditional food from Chile

Ingredients

  • Hot Dog Bun
  • Tomatoes
  • Avocado
  • Hot dog sausage
  • Mayo

Steps

  1. Firt cook the sausage while toasting the bun
  2. Cut the tomatoes in small chunks and mash the avocado
  3. Put the sausage in the bun, top with tomatoe avocado and lastly mayo.
